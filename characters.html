Characters of To Kill a Mockingbird

  1. Jean Louise "Scout" Finch - Narrator and protagonist of the story who, just like her father, defy's what society deems to be correct.

  2. Jeremy "Jem" Finch - Scout's older brother and constant playmate.

  3. Atticus Finch - Father to Jem and Scout. Believes in equality and has a great role in his children's lives. He defy's society and stands up for what he believes is right. He defends Tom Robinson as a lawyer.

  4. Charles Baker "Dill" Harris - Jem and Scout's summer neighbor and friend who feels neglected by his family and runs away to Maycomb.

  5. Arthur "Boo" Radley - Symbolizes a mockingbird and is a mysterious entity for the most part of the novel who fascinates Jem, Dill and Scout. He is just as fascinated by them as they are by him.

  6. Tom Robinson - Victim of social prejudice. Atticu's client during trial.
